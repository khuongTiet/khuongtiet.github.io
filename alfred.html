Background

The main inspiration for developing Alfred came from when I would often look at websites of previous courses to look at the notes and syllabus. It was increasingly tedious to have to individually click and download each file on the page, with some pages having upwards of 60 files to download. I had originally considered just writing a simple script that would take in a URL as a parameter and compile the downloads for me but I thought it better to actually develop a web tool that could potential help other people facing a similar problem.
